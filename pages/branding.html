Section Fourteen

Personal Brand Building

Fashion tech is a small world. Visibility matters more here than in big tech.

What to do

  1. Pick a niche angle: "AI in sustainable fashion", "3D digital design", "fashion recommendation systems"
  2. Post weekly on LinkedIn: Share a fashion tech insight, company analysis, or project update
  3. Build 1 public project: Something people can see and share -- a trend analyzer, outfit recommender, virtual try-on demo
  4. Attend 1 event per month: Virtual or in-person
  5. Connect with 5 new people per week: Engage with their content first. Don't cold pitch

The payoff: recruiters find you, you get invited to events, you build credibility before interviews, and you meet potential collaborators.
