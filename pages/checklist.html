Section Nine

Action Checklist

This is specific on purpose. Vague goals don't get done.

Phase 1: Orientation (Week 1 - 2)

  • Download and read State of Fashion 2025/2026 from McKinsey/BoF (free PDF)
  • Download and read State of Fashion: Technology report
  • Subscribe to The Interline, Glossy, BoF, and Vogue Business newsletters
  • Follow these 11 people on LinkedIn (listed in Section 7)
  • Open every company website in Section 2. Spend 5 minutes each. Write one sentence about what they do
  • Listen to 5 episodes of Glossy Podcast or Loose Threads
  • Pick your top 3 subsectors from Section 1
  • Make a spreadsheet: Company | Role That Fits | Why It Interests You

Phase 2: Skill Up (Month 1)

  • Be honest: where are you on technical skills, design skills, fashion knowledge? Write it down
  • Pick your lane:
    • Technical: Start fast.ai Practical Deep Learning (free)
    • Creative: Start CLO3D tutorials on YouTube, then paid course
    • Business: Start BoF Professional courses
  • Set up Python. Do the Fashion-MNIST tutorial end to end
  • Download and explore the DeepFashion dataset
  • Join the Fashion and Technology LinkedIn group and The Interline community
  • Update your LinkedIn headline to signal fashion tech interest

Phase 3: Build Portfolio (Month 2 - 3)

  • Complete 2 portfolio projects from Section 8 (one beginner, one intermediate)
  • Write 1 blog post about what you built and what you learned
  • Launch a portfolio website (even a simple one-page site counts)
  • Find one open-source fashion tech project on GitHub and contribute
  • Write a proper case study for your best project

Phase 4: Network and Apply (Month 3 - 6)

  • Attend 1 fashion tech event (Fashion Tech Forum, or any from Section 7)
  • Send 5 LinkedIn messages to people in fashion tech asking for 20-minute informational interviews. Be specific about why you're reaching out to them
  • Apply to 10+ roles at companies from Section 2
  • Prepare a 10-minute portfolio presentation (visual, tells a story)
  • Practice 3 case study interviews from Section 10
  • Apply to 1 hackathon from the list
  • Write your "Why fashion tech?" story in 3 sentences. Memorize it
  • Tailor your resume for each application. Generic resumes get ignored
