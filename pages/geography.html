Remote-Friendly Companies

  • Most startups (Cala, Heuritech, Lily AI) offer remote/hybrid
  • ASOS, Zalando, Farfetch have remote engineering roles
  • Freelance/contract work is common, especially creative-technical roles

Where to Start

  • NYC for maximum options and network density
  • LA if you lean creative, DTC, influencer economy
  • London for luxury + sustainability
  • Remote is viable. Many fashion tech companies are distributed
