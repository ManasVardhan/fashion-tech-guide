Industry Overview

People who speak both fashion and tech are rare. That's where the opportunity is.

Fashion tech covers how software, hardware, and data change the way clothes get designed, made, marketed, and sold.

Market Size

  • Fashion e-commerce: ~$1T+ (2025)
  • Fashion AI market: projected $4.4B by 2027 (Allied Market Research)
  • Resale market: $350B+ globally by 2027 (ThredUp Resale Report)
  • Digital fashion/virtual goods: emerging multi-billion market
