Books

Read first

  • Fashionopolis by Dana Thomas -- sustainability + tech in fashion. Best single book for context
  • The End of Fashion by Teri Agins -- classic on industry transformation

Read when relevant

  • Digital Luxury by Wided Batat -- if you're going luxury
  • Fashion and Technology by Aneta Genova, Katherine Moriwaki -- academic but useful
  • Designing with Smart Textiles by Sarah Kettley -- if wearables interest you
  • Artificial Intelligence for Fashion Industry in the Big Data Era by Thomassey and Zeng -- academic, Springer
  • Worn by Sofi Thanhauser -- deep cultural context
  • Deluxe by Dana Thomas -- luxury business
  • Overdressed by Elizabeth Cline -- fast fashion critique
  • The Fashion System by Roland Barthes -- semiotics (academic)

Industry Reports

Must-read (free)

  • The State of Fashion (annual) -- McKinsey and BoF. Download this first
  • The State of Fashion: Technology -- McKinsey and BoF
  • Resale Report (annual) -- ThredUp
  • Fashion Transparency Index -- Fashion Revolution

Worth it if you can access

  • Technology in Fashion -- BoF Insights (paid)
  • Luxury Fashion Market Monitor -- Bain and Company / Altagamma
  • Global Fashion Agenda reports -- GFA
