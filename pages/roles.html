Section Three

Roles in Fashion Tech

Technical Roles

ML Engineer (Recommendation Systems / Computer Vision)

  • What you do: Build algorithms for product recs, visual search, trend prediction, virtual try-on
  • Skills: Python, PyTorch/TensorFlow, computer vision (CNNs, GANs, diffusion models), recommendation systems, NLP, MLOps
  • Salary: $130K -- $220K (US), 60K -- 120K GBP (UK)
  • Entry path: CS/ML degree, ML internship, then specialize in fashion
  • Who's hiring: Stitch Fix, Amazon Fashion, Zalando, Farfetch, Lily AI, Heuritech

Data Scientist (Fashion)

  • What you do: Analyze trends, customer behavior, pricing, demand forecasting, A/B testing
  • Skills: Python, SQL, statistics, experimentation, data viz, fashion domain knowledge
  • Salary: $110K -- $180K (US)
  • Entry path: Quantitative degree into data science, then fashion vertical
  • Who's hiring: Stitch Fix, ASOS, Zalando, Revolve, any fashion e-commerce

Frontend / UX Engineer (Fashion Apps)

  • What you do: Build fashion e-commerce experiences, product pages, try-on interfaces
  • Skills: React/Next.js, TypeScript, mobile dev, animation, performance, strong design eye
  • Salary: $100K -- $180K (US)
  • Entry path: Frontend experience + portfolio with design-oriented work
  • Who's hiring: SSENSE, ASOS, Farfetch, Shopify

AR Developer

  • What you do: Build virtual try-on, AR mirrors, AR filters for fashion
  • Skills: ARKit/ARCore, Unity/Unreal, Snap Lens Studio, 3D graphics, shader programming, body tracking
  • Salary: $110K -- $190K (US)
  • Entry path: Graphics/game dev background, then AR specialization
  • Who's hiring: Snap, Zero10, Obsess, Wanna, Perfect Corp, Google

3D Fashion Designer / Technical Designer

  • What you do: Create digital garments, virtual samples, 3D prototypes
  • Skills: CLO3D, Browzwear VStitcher, Marvelous Designer, Blender, garment construction knowledge
  • Salary: $60K -- $120K (US)
  • Entry path: Fashion design degree + 3D software, or 3D artist transitioning to fashion
  • Who's hiring: The Fabricant, DressX, PVH, Adidas, Nike

Creative-Technical Hybrid Roles

Creative Technologist

  • What you do: Bridge creative and engineering. Prototype AR, interactive retail, digital fashion experiences
  • Skills: Coding (JS/Python), prototyping, creative tools, storytelling
  • Salary: $90K -- $160K (US)
  • Entry path: Design + tech background with experimental project portfolio
  • Who's hiring: Nike, Burberry, agencies (AKQA, R/GA), The Fabricant

Digital Fashion Designer

  • What you do: Design garments that exist only digitally -- gaming, social media, metaverse
  • Skills: CLO3D, Blender, Substance Painter, fashion design fundamentals, gaming/avatar platforms
  • Salary: $50K -- $100K (US, varies widely -- emerging field)
  • Entry path: Fashion design + 3D skills. Build portfolio on DressX
  • Who's hiring: The Fabricant, DressX, gaming companies, brand digital teams

Fashion Data Analyst / Trend Forecasting Analyst

  • What you do: Use data to spot emerging trends, inform design/buying decisions
  • Skills: SQL, Python/R, data viz (Tableau, Looker), fashion knowledge
  • Salary: $65K -- $110K (US)
  • Entry path: Analytics + fashion interest, or fashion + analytics skills
  • Who's hiring: Heuritech, Edited, Trendalytics, Stylumia

Product Manager (Fashion Tech)

  • What you do: Define strategy for recommendation engines, try-on, search, personalization
  • Skills: PM fundamentals, data literacy, UX understanding, fashion domain knowledge
  • Salary: $110K -- $180K (US)
  • Entry path: PM in e-commerce/consumer tech, then fashion. Or fashion industry + MBA/product bootcamp
  • Who's hiring: Stitch Fix, Farfetch, Zalando, ASOS

Non-Technical but Tech-Adjacent

Fashion Tech Consultant

  • Skills: Fashion industry knowledge, tech landscape awareness, strategic thinking
  • Salary: $80K -- $150K (US); higher at big consultancies
  • Entry path: Consulting + fashion focus, or fashion + MBA
  • Who's hiring: McKinsey (BoF partnership), BCG, Bain, Accenture (fashion and luxury practice)

Digital Merchandiser

  • Skills: Merchandising fundamentals, analytics, A/B testing, CMS platforms
  • Salary: $55K -- $100K (US)
  • Who's hiring: Nordstrom, Net-a-Porter, ASOS, Shopify merchants

Sustainability Analyst (Fashion)

  • Skills: Sustainability frameworks (GRI, SASB), LCA analysis, supply chain knowledge
  • Salary: $60K -- $100K (US)
  • Who's hiring: Good On You, EON, Provenance, Kering, H&M
